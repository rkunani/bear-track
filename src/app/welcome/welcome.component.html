Don't you hate it when you try to enroll in a class, but there aren't any spots available when your enrollment time comes?

Wouldn't enrollment be less stressful if you didn't have to keep checking whether a class has an open seat?

Sign up with BearTrack to get a text when that class has an open seat, so you don't have to check yourself!



How it Works

  1. Sign up with your name and cell phone number
  2. Tell BearTrack which class you want it track for you
  3. Get a text when that class is open or closed (you choose)
