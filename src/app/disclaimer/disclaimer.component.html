Disclaimers

  • BearTrack considers a class as having an open seat if classes.berkeley.edu reports the class as having open seats
  • If BearTrack notifies you that there are open seats in a class, you may still not be able to enroll (due to reserve seat policy, course enrollment permissions, or holds on your enrollment)
  • BearTrack pulls enrollment information every 5 minutes from the same database that backs classes.berkeley.edu
